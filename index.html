Sales Process

To ensure fairness and transparency for all interested parties, Ranger Estate follows a structured sales procedure. Below are the key terms and conditions that will guide your purchase process.

Letter of Offer Process

  • • Each allotment will be offered for sale by letter of offer
  • • All interested parties must provide the selling consultant a copy of the letter on or before the close of business on (date to be agreed upon)
  • • A "LETTER OF OFFER TO PURCHASE" will be executed by purchaser to confirm commitment to contract on a particular allotment
  • • No land holds will be taken during the day of release

Offer Priority & Conditions

  • • Unconditional offers of purchase will receive priority over those subject to conditions
  • • House and Land Finance will not be accepted or considered
  • • Land only conditions will receive consideration from the vendor
  • • Offers will be presented to the vendor and all parties advised of their success or otherwise as soon as possible

Financial Terms

  • • A minimum deposit of 10% unless otherwise agreed by the vendor will be required at the expiration of the cooling off period
  • • Settlement of land to occur ? days from deposit of plan and issue of titles

Important Notes

  • • This summary has been provided in good faith to ensure all interested parties are clear on the procedure and terms of offering
  • • If you are uncertain on any issue it is important to talk to the Consultant
